## **Module Overview**

This comprehensive training module is designed to educate candidates on how to participate effectively in elections using the VoteAPI platform. It covers the nomination process, campaign guidelines, compliance requirements, and candidate responsibilities.

## **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this training, candidates will be able to:
- Understand the nomination and approval process
- Create effective campaign materials
- Comply with campaign rules and regulations
- Understand election procedures and timeline
- Navigate the candidate portal effectively
- Handle disputes and appeals
- Understand result interpretation and acceptance

## **Section 2: Candidate Registration Process (Admin-Added)**

### **2.1 How Candidate Registration Works**

#### **Important: Candidates Cannot Self-Register**
- **Candidates DO NOT register themselves** in the system
- **Only admins can add candidates** to the system
- **Candidates submit documents to admin** first
- **Admin reviews and adds approved candidates**

#### **Step-by-Step Process**
1. **Document Submission**: Candidate submits required documents to admin
2. **Admin Review**: Admin reviews documents and eligibility
3. **Admin Decision**: Admin approves or rejects candidate
4. **System Addition**: Admin adds approved candidate to system
5. **Position Assignment**: Admin assigns candidate to specific position
6. **Profile Creation**: System generates candidate profile
7. **Notification**: Candidate receives confirmation

### **2.2 Document Submission Process**

#### **Required Documents**
- **Application Form**: Complete nomination application
- **Supporting Documents**: Required supporting documents
- **Identification**: Valid identification
- **Academic Records**: Academic transcripts
- **Experience Documentation**: Experience documentation
- **References**: Reference letters

#### **Submission Method**
- **Submit to Admin**: Submit documents directly to election admin
- **Not Online**: Do not submit through the system
- **Physical/Email**: Submit via email or physical delivery
- **Deadline**: Submit before admin-set deadline
- **Confirmation**: Get receipt confirmation from admin

### **2.3 Admin Review Process**

#### **Review Timeline**
- **Document Review**: Admin reviews within 24-48 hours
- **Eligibility Check**: Admin verifies eligibility criteria
- **Decision**: Admin makes approval/rejection decision
- **Notification**: Admin notifies candidate of decision
- **System Addition**: Admin adds approved candidates to system

#### **Review Criteria**
- **Eligibility**: Meet all position requirements
- **Documentation**: Complete and accurate documents
- **Experience**: Meet experience requirements
- **References**: Valid reference letters
- **Compliance**: Follow all nomination rules

### **2.4 What Happens After Approval**

#### **System Profile Creation**
- **Admin adds candidate** to the system
- **System generates profile** automatically
- **Position assignment** by admin
- **Candidate credentials** sent to candidate
- **Portal access** provided to candidate

#### **Candidate Responsibilities After Approval**
- **Access Portal**: Login to candidate portal
- **Complete Profile**: Complete candidate profile
- **Upload Materials**: Upload campaign materials
- **Schedule Events**: Schedule campaign events
- **Monitor Compliance**: Monitor campaign compliance

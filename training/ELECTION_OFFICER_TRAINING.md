## **Module Overview**

This comprehensive training module is designed for Election Officers to understand their roles, responsibilities, and procedures for conducting fair and transparent elections on the VoteAPI platform.

## **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this training, election officers will be able to:
- Understand their role in the election process
- Review and approve candidate nominations
- Monitor campaign activities and compliance
- Assist voters with voting issues
- Handle disputes and resolve conflicts
- Monitor election integrity and security
- Ensure fair and transparent elections

## **Section 1: Role and Responsibilities**

### **1.1 Election Officer Role**

#### **Primary Responsibilities**
- **Candidate Review**: Review and approve candidate nominations
- **Campaign Monitoring**: Oversee campaign activities and compliance
- **Voter Assistance**: Help voters with issues and questions
- **Dispute Resolution**: Handle voting and candidate disputes
- **Election Monitoring**: Monitor election day activities
- **Integrity Assurance**: Ensure election integrity and fairness

#### **Authority and Limits**
- **Approval Authority**: Approve/reject candidate nominations
- **Campaign Oversight**: Monitor campaign compliance
- **Voter Support**: Assist voters with technical issues
- **Dispute Handling**: Resolve minor disputes
- **Reporting**: Report issues to election managers
- **No Authority**: Cannot modify election settings or results

### **1.2 Code of Conduct**

#### **Impartiality Requirements**
- **Neutral Stance**: Remain neutral in all election matters
- **No Endorsements**: Cannot endorse candidates
- **Fair Treatment**: Treat all candidates equally
- **Objective Decisions**: Make decisions based on facts
- **Conflict Avoidance**: Avoid conflicts of interest
- **Professional Conduct**: Maintain professional standards

#### **Ethical Standards**
- **Integrity**: Maintain highest integrity standards
- **Transparency**: Be transparent in all actions
- **Accountability**: Take responsibility for decisions
- **Respect**: Respect all stakeholders
- **Confidentiality**: Maintain confidentiality when required
- **Compliance**: Follow all rules and regulations
